Dashboard Worksheets

Seattle Revenue Map Seasonal Price Dual-Axis Bedrooms Price Matrix Detailed List View
  • Seattle Revenue Choropleth Map: Visualizes average booking price by ZIP code, highlighting premium areas vs budget-conscious zones across the metropolitan area.
